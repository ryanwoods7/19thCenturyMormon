19thCenturyMormon.com is a newly established website made by two civil engineers, Matt George and Ryan Woods, which shows events of the early Mormon Saints in the 19th Century. 19thCenturyMromon.com uses a Bing map to show early events such as the organization of the church, restoration of the Aaronic and Melchizedek Priesthood, publication of the Book of Mormon, the settling of different areas in North America, the Mormon Trail from Nauvoo to Salt Lake City, the Adam-ondi-Ahman prophecy, the Liberty Jail and Martyrdom of Joseph Smith, and the dedication of the St. George Temple (the oldest temple still running today). Our site is interactive with popup bubbles for each of the sites and a scroll menu for quick access to the sites. If you wish to learn more about a particular topic, click on the topic and in the bubble click on the link at the bottom. This will lead you to a website with more information on the topic. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us under the Contact tab.
